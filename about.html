About Me

As a Registered Nurse and Foot Health Care Practitioner, I bring over two decades of experience in caring for patients and supporting their wellbeing.

Meet Antony


As a qualified Foot Health Care Practitioner and Registered Nurse with over 20 years of clinical experience, I am passionate about delivering high-quality, personalised foot care tailored to each patient’s individual needs. I provide professional treatment in a calm, friendly, and supportive environment, helping patients improve comfort, mobility, and overall foot health. By combining clinical expertise with a compassionate approach, I ensure every patient receives dedicated care and treatment plans focused on promoting long-term health, wellbeing, and confidence. Throughout my nursing career, I have gained extensive experience caring for patients with a wide range of health conditions, including diabetes, reduced mobility, circulatory concerns, and age-related foot problems. This background allows me to recognise the importance of early intervention and preventative care in maintaining healthy feet and overall wellbeing.


I have experience providing routine foot care treatments such as nail trimming, corn and callus reduction, thickened nail management, and diabetic foot assessments. I understand that foot discomfort can have a significant impact on daily life, mobility, and independence, and I am committed to helping patients remain active and comfortable. My approach is centred on building trust and ensuring every patient feels listened to, respected, and fully supported throughout their treatment.
